Hi, I'm Wendy!

I'm a tenure-track associate professor with the Capital Normal University in Beijing, China!

I was a postdoctoral research fellow with the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at Duke University, Durham, NC, USA from 2017 to 2019. I received the B.S. degree in Computer Science and Technology from Minzu University of China, Beijing, China in 2010 and my Ph.D. degree in Computer Architecture from Institute of Computing Technology, University of Chinese Academy of Sciences in 2016.

Research Interests:

  • Accelerator for data-intensive applications
  • In/Near memory computing
  • Memory system
